Research

My research direction centers on extracting robust physics from challenging collider topologies. I also interest in the software and infrastructure development, allowing efficient analysis workflows in large collaborations.

Core Themes

  • Quantum-correlation measurements in H -> WW* -> lvlv
  • W-rest-frame reconstruction in under-constrained final states
  • Physics-constrained, flow-based, and transformer ML models for event inference
  • Higgs and electroweak couplings analysis
  • HGTD production database website development

Technical Stack

  • ML: PyTorch/Lightning, FrEIA, ONNX, TensorFlow/Keras
  • HEP: ROOT, FastFrames, uproot, mplhep
  • Systems: Python, C++, UNIX, Git, Docker/Podman, CernVM-FS
