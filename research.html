Research

My research program focuses on extracting robust physics measurements from challenging collider topologies. In parallel, I develop software and analysis infrastructure that improves reproducibility and workflow efficiency in large collaborations.

Core Themes

  • Quantum-correlation measurements in $H \to WW^* \to \ell\nu\ell\nu$
  • W rest-frame reconstruction in under-constrained final states
  • Physics-constrained transformer and flow-based models for event inference
  • Higgs and electroweak coupling studies
  • HGTD production-database platform development

Technical Stack

  • ML: PyTorch/Lightning, FrEIA, ONNX, TensorFlow/Keras
  • HEP: ROOT, FastFrames, uproot, mplhep
  • Systems: Python, C++, UNIX, Git, Docker/Podman, CVMFS
