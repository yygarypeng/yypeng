Projects

ML Event Reconstruction

I develop transformer, flow-based, and physics-constrained models to recover missing information in the $H \to WW^* \to \ell\nu\ell\nu$ final state, improving key-observable resolution and measurement sensitivity.

The pipeline is built for ATLAS analysis adoption (ONNX format), with reproducible training, systematic stress tests, and channel-consistent validation.

Quantum Correlations

Entanglement in high-energy collisions is a fundamental aspect of quantum theory, yet it remains underexplored. My work is among the first to study quantum correlations in $H \to WW^* \to \ell\nu\ell\nu$.

My main contributions are:

  • Design neural networks to reconstruct W-rest-frame variables and enable density-matrix measurements.
  • Study W-boson polarizations and quantum-correlation observables defined in the density matrix.

Entanglement reference: New Scientist article.

HGTD Production Database

Co-develop frontend and backend components for HGTD production-database webpage, helping teams organize, query, and visualize detector information efficiently.

Run3 HWW Coupling

Co-mentor two MSc students and jointly study electroweak couplings in $H \to WW^*$ with Run 3 data, providing analysis guidance. In parallel, I contribute portable analysis environments and workflow tooling that reduce setup friction across collaborators.
