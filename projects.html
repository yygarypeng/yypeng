Projects

I develop transformer, flow-based, and physics-constrained models to recover the missing information in the H -> WW* -> lvlv final state, improving the resolution of key observables and the sensitivity of physics measurements.

The pipeline is built for ATLAS analysis adoption (ONNX format), with reproducible training, systematic stress tests, and channel-consistent validation.

Quantum Correlations

Entanglement studies in high energy is important corner stone on quantum nature but rarely discussed. My work is among the first to explore the quantum correlation in H -> WW* -> lvlv.

My main contributions are

  • Design neural networks to reconstruct the W-rest frame variables, enabling to measure the density matrix of W bosons
  • Study the polariztions of W bosons and the quantum-correlation observables defined in the density matrix

Entanglement reference: New Scientist article.
