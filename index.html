Yuan-Yen Peng

I am currently serving in Research and Development Substitute Service (RDSS) at National Tsing Hua University, contributing to the ATLAS experiment. I have more than four years of experience in experimental particle physics, with a focus on Higgs physics, quantum-correlation measurements at the LHC, machine-learning-based event reconstruction, and production-database development for the High Granularity Timing Detector (HGTD).

Professional Summary

My current program combines quantum-correlation measurements in H -> WW* -> lvlv, W-rest-frame reconstruction in under-constrained final states, and software development for the ATLAS High Granularity Timing Detector (HGTD). I prioritize reproducibility, systematic robustness, and practical integration into collaboration workflows.
