ATLAS HWW Reconstruction, W Polarization, and Physics-Guided ML

I am Yuan-Yen Peng, an ATLAS HWW researcher working across reconstruction, inference, ML, and detector-aware workflows. My work connects \(H \to WW^* \to \ell\nu\ell\nu\), W-polarization interpretation, Run 3 validation, HGTD database work, and reproducible ATLAS/ML environments. I am looking for PhD projects where methods serve concrete physics output.
