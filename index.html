Physics and Beyond

I am Yuan-Yen Peng, an applicant for the ATLAS experimental particle physics program. Looking toward the future of collider physics, I envision a supportive partnership among physics, detectors, and machine learning. Physics raises questions and provides essential context, detectors create unique environments, and ML harnesses cutting-edge methodologies to uncover answers and drive groundbreaking discoveries. I aspire to enhance ATLAS's impact by delivering deep insights through precise measurements, creating state-of-the-art deployable tools, and facilitating robust collaborative efforts. Here is my CV.
