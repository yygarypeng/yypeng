Yuan-Yen Peng

Perceiving the beauty of the universe, analyzing the principles of nature.

I am Yuan-Yen Peng, an ATLAS physicist currently working on \(H \to WW^* \to \ell\nu\ell\nu\) quantum-information and coupling analyses, with a focus on ML-assisted reconstruction and W-polarization studies. I also develop the HGTD production database for the Phase-II upgrade.

I am seeking PhD projects where reconstruction, event discrimination, inference, or detector-aware methods enable a concrete ATLAS physics result. Here is my CV.

At a Glance

Physics

Current work in HWW coupling and quantum-information analyses, with precision measurements and BSM as a future direction.

Reconstruction

Physics-constrained, invertible, and attention-based inference of boson (neutrino) kinematics in partially observed events, with adaptive multi-loss training.

Validation

Shape, bias, resolution, truth-to-reconstruction, SR/CR, central analysis framework integration and reproducible workflows.

ATLAS readiness

Run 3 analysis work, HGTD database experience, and reproducible ROOT, Python, and containerized workflows.
